About RideLink

RideLink Concierge bridges the gap between drivers and riders by ensuring reliability, comfort, and reduced waiting times. We help riders enjoy safe, pre-booked rides, and drivers earn more by reaching serious customers — all through a simple WhatsApp-based booking system.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Riders

1. What is RideLink and how is it different from Uber or Bolt?

RideLink lets you pre-book trusted, vetted drivers via WhatsApp. Unlike Uber/Bolt, you avoid long waits and last-minute cancellations.

2. How do I book a ride?

Just send us a WhatsApp message with your pickup, destination, date, and time. We'll confirm and assign a verified driver.

3. Can I book for someone else?

Yes, you can book for friends, family, children, or employees — just provide their details.

4. Is there a mobile app?

No app is needed — everything runs smoothly through WhatsApp.

5. Can I trust the drivers?

Yes. All RideLink drivers are screened with identity checks, vehicle verification, and customer ratings.

6. What kind of vehicles are used?

Only neat, air-conditioned cars — sedans, SUVs, etc. No faulty or substandard vehicles.

7. Is RideLink available 24/7?

Yes, but advance booking is encouraged to ensure prompt service.

8. How much does a typical ride cost?

Fares vary by distance and time. You’ll get a quote before confirming a ride.

9. Can I cancel a booked ride?

Yes, but late cancellations may attract a small fee if a driver was already dispatched.

10. How do I report a bad experience?

Message our WhatsApp support or use the “Report Issue” button on our site.

For Drivers

1. Can I join RideLink while driving for Uber, Bolt, or LagRide?

Yes! RideLink is not a competitor. We give you additional bookings and steady income.

2. How do I sign up as a driver?

Click “Join as Driver” and you’ll be added to our onboarding WhatsApp group.

3. What documents are required?

Driver’s license, vehicle papers, insurance, and photos of your car.

4. What type of cars are accepted?

Neat, air-conditioned cars only — preferably 2008 models and above.

5. How are trips assigned to me?

We send requests via WhatsApp. If you’re available, confirm and get connected.

6. Is there a subscription or registration fee?

No. For now, RideLink only takes a small service fee per trip.

7. Can I set my availability hours?

Yes, you choose your working hours — mornings, evenings, or weekends.

8. How do I get paid?

Riders pay you directly — cash or transfer. No middleman delays.

9. What if a rider cancels late?

Verified late cancellations may still come with partial compensation.

10. Is there driver support?

Yes. Our support team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp and phone.
